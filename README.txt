## Features

### Deck
- **Red Deck**: Gives 10 discards.
- **Blue Deck**: Gives 10 hands.
- **Yellow Deck**: Gives a 999,999,999 cash.
- **Joker Slots**: Every deck now provides 45 joker slots.

### Jokers
- Every Joker is OP now lol(yes, all 150 jokers)
- **Joker Abilities**:
  Joker abilities that are related to chance will be guaranteed(except for debuff)
  Jokers that retrigger cards now retrigger them **5 times** (instead of just once).

## Editions

- **Foil Edition**: Grants **999,999,999 chips**.  
- **Holographic Edition**: Multiplies your base score by **999,999,999**.  
- **Polychrome Edition**: Multiplies your current multiplier by **99**.  
- **Negative Edition**: Provides **10 extra joker slots**.
